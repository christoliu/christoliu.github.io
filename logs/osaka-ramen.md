# Eat 'til you drop (Kuidaore)

Osaka is known as the nation's kitchen. Our mission: find the best bowl of ramen.

## Contender 1: Kinryu Ramen
The one with the giant dragon.
*   **Broth**: Light Tonkotsu
*   **Noodles**: Standard
*   **Verdict**: Good for late night, but not the best.

## Contender 2: Hanamaruken
Slow cooked pork rib ramen.
*   **The Rib**: Fall-off-the-bone tender.
*   **Verdict**: **Winner!**
